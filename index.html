Our Coffee Journey

From Bean to Cup

Founded in 2015, Mekar Harum began with a simple mission: to bring the finest coffee from Indonesian highlands to coffee lovers worldwide.

We work directly with small-scale farmers in Sumatra, Java, and Bali to source the highest quality Arabica and Robusta beans. Our master roaster carefully crafts each batch to highlight the unique characteristics of each origin.
